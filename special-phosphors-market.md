<p><p>The special phosphors market is segmented primarily into UV phosphors and infrared phosphors. UV phosphors are essential for applications in UV lights and fluorescent tubes, converting ultraviolet energy into visible light, thus enhancing efficiency and performance in various lighting solutions. In contrast, infrared phosphors are used in applications such as infrared sensors and thermal imaging, converting infrared radiation into visible light. Both types of phosphors are crucial in industries like consumer electronics, telecommunications, and healthcare, driving innovation and technology advancements.</p></p>
