<p><p>An Air Cooled Heat Exchanger (ACHE) is a type of heat exchanger that uses air as the cooling medium to remove heat from a process fluid. It typically consists of finned tubes that maximize the surface area for heat transfer, allowing for efficient cooling without the use of water. ACHEs are widely used across various industries, including oil and gas, chemical processing, power generation, and HVAC.</p><p>The Air Cooled Heat Exchanger Market is experiencing significant growth driven by increasing demand for energy efficiency and sustainability in industrial processes.
