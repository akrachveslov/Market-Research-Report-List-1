<p><p>The air purifier market encompasses several types, each utilizing distinct technologies. HEPA filters capture fine particles, effectively trapping allergens and dust. Activated carbon filters remove odors and volatile organic compounds through adsorption. UV technology uses ultraviolet light to eliminate bacteria and viruses. Ionizers release negatively charged ions to attract and settle airborne particles. Ozone generators produce ozone to neutralize pollutants, though their safety is debated. Other types include electrostatic and photolytic air purifiers, catering to varying air quality needs.</p></p>
