<p><p>The biogas upgrading market features several methods to enhance biogas quality. Membrane separation utilizes selective barriers to separate methane from contaminants. Water scrubbing employs water to absorb carbon dioxide and other impurities, retaining methane. Chemical scrubbing uses chemical solvents to selectively absorb unwanted gases. Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) operates by cycling pressure to separate methane from other gases through adsorption. Additional methods may include cryogenic techniques and amine scrubbing, enhancing biogas for renewable energy applications.</p></p>
