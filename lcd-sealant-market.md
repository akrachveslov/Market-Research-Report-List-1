<p><strong>The LCD Sealant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Component</li><li>Two-Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LCD sealant market is categorized into single-component and two-component types. Single-component sealants are ready to use upon application and cure through exposure to moisture or heat, making them convenient for quick jobs. In contrast, two-component sealants require the mixing of a base and hardener before application, offering enhanced strength and durability once cured. Each type serves different needs, with single-component favored for simplicity and speed, while two-component sealants are preferred for demanding applications requiring superior performance.</p></p>
