<p><ul><li>Active Piezo Buzzer</li><li>Passive Piezo Buzzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezo buzzers market is divided into two main types: active and passive piezo buzzers. Active piezo buzzers incorporate an internal oscillator that generates sound when voltage is applied, making them easy to use with simple connections. In contrast, passive piezo buzzers require an external audio signal to produce sound, allowing for more versatile sound production but necessitating additional components. Both types find applications in consumer electronics, alarms, and various signaling devices, catering to different sound generation needs.</p></p>
