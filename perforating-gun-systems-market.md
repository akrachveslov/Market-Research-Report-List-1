<p><strong>The Perforating Gun Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>WCP</li><li>TCP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Perforating gun systems are crucial in oil and gas exploration, primarily categorized into Wireline Conveyed Perforators (WCP) and Tubing Conveyed Perforators (TCP). WCP involves deploying perforating guns via wireline for controlled and precise operations, typically in open hole completion. In contrast, TCP utilizes existing tubing to deliver perforating guns, allowing for high-pressure environments and deeper formations. Both systems enhance well productivity but cater to different operational needs and environmental conditions, offering flexibility in perforation strategies.</p></p>
