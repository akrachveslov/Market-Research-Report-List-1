<p><ul><li>One-finger Touch Measurement</li><li>Two-finger Pressure Measurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ECG smartwatch market encompasses two primary types of measurement methods: one-finger touch measurement and two-finger pressure measurement. One-finger touch measurement allows users to take ECG readings by placing a single finger on the smartwatch's sensor, making it convenient and quick. In contrast, two-finger pressure measurement requires users to apply pressure with two fingers, providing potentially more accurate readings by capturing a broader electrical signal from the heart. Both methods cater to the growing demand for accessible cardiovascular health monitoring.</p></p>
