<p><p>Third-party logistics (3PL) market types include B2B, B2C, and C2C. B2B (Business-to-Business) logistics focus on transporting goods between companies, optimizing supply chains for manufacturers and wholesalers. B2C (Business-to-Consumer) logistics cater to retail businesses, delivering products directly to consumers, often emphasizing speed and convenience. In contrast, C2C (Consumer-to-Consumer) logistics involve individual sellers and buyers, typically facilitated by online platforms, where 3PL providers help manage deliveries, thus enhancing the overall efficiency of peer-to-peer transactions.</p></p>
