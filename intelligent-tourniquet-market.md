<p><p>The Intelligent Tourniquet market is primarily categorized into Single Port and Dual Port types. Single Port tourniquets utilize one access point for fluid management and pressure control, offering simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, Dual Port tourniquets feature two access points, allowing for enhanced functionality, such as simultaneous monitoring and manipulation of multiple fluid pathways. This differentiation caters to varying clinical needs, with Single Port models being favored for streamlined procedures, while Dual Port models provide versatility and comprehensive assessment capabilities in complex scenarios.</p></p>
