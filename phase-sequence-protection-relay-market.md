<p><p>Phase sequence protection relays are essential for safeguarding three-phase systems from phase imbalances and incorrect sequencing. The regular type simply detects phase sequence faults to prevent motor damage. The type with undervoltage protection adds an extra layer of security by disconnecting the circuit when voltage drops below a set threshold, protecting equipment during voltage sags. Other variants may incorporate features like built-in timers, status indicators, or communication capabilities to enhance monitoring and control, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
