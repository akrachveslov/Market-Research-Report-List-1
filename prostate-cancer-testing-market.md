<p><p>The prostate cancer testing market comprises various methods for early detection and diagnosis. PSA tests measure prostate-specific antigen levels in the blood, indicating potential cancer presence. CTC tests identify circulating tumor cells, aiding in assessing disease progression. Immunohistochemistry examines tissue samples for specific cancer markers. PCA3 tests measure mRNA levels linked to prostate cancer, enhancing diagnostic accuracy. Other methods include imaging and genetic testing, collectively providing a comprehensive approach to prostate cancer detection and monitoring, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
