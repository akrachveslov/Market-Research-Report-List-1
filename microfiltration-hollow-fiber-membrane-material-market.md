<p><p>The microfiltration hollow-fiber membrane material market is categorized into three main types: PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) filtration membranes, PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) filtration membranes, and others. PVDF membranes are favored for their chemical resistance and durability, ideal for various water treatment applications. PTFE membranes are known for superior hydrophobicity and temperature resistance, often utilized in applications involving aggressive solvents. Other materials may include polyamide and polysulfone membranes, each offering specific benefits like enhanced permeability or selective filtration capabilities across diverse industries.</p></p>
