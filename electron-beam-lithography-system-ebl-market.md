<p><p>Electron Beam Lithography (EBL) is a high-precision technique used for patterning at the nanoscale, essential for applications in semiconductor fabrication, nanotechnology, and advanced materials. The market includes two primary types: Gaussian beam EBL systems, which utilize a focused Gaussian profile to achieve high resolution but limited writing speed, and shaped beam EBL systems, which employ customized beam shapes to enhance throughput and improve patterning efficiency for complex geometries. Both systems cater to varying industrial needs in advanced manufacturing.</p></p>
