<p><ul><li>Quantum dots</li><li>Nanoparticles</li><li>Nanoshells</li><li>Nanotubes</li><li>Nanodevices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nanomedicine market encompasses various types of nanomaterials used in medical applications. Quantum dots are semiconductor particles utilized for imaging and diagnostics due to their fluorescent properties. Nanoparticles serve as drug delivery systems, enhancing the efficacy and targeting of therapeutics. Nanoshells, consisting of a dielectric core surrounded by a metallic shell, are valuable in photothermal therapy. Nanotubes, known for their strength and conductivity, are explored in drug delivery and biosensing. Finally, nanodevices integrate nanoscale components for diagnostics and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
