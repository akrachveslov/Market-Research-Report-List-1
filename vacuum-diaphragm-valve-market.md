<p><p>The vacuum diaphragm valve market consists of three types based on the vacuum level they can achieve. Low vacuum valves operate in a pressure range typically from atmospheric pressure to around 1 torr, suited for general applications. High vacuum valves function in the 1 torr to 10^-6 torr range, ideal for advanced industrial processes. Ultra-high vacuum valves operate below 10^-6 torr, essential for scientific research and semiconductor manufacturing, where extremely low pressure is crucial for optimal performance and purity.</p></p>
