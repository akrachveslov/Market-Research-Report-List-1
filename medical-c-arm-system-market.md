<p><strong>The Medical C Arm System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Mobile C-arm</li><li>FPD Mobile C-arm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical C-Arm System market primarily consists of two types: CCD Mobile C-arms and FPD Mobile C-arms. CCD Mobile C-arms utilize charge-coupled device technology, offering high-quality imaging for surgical and diagnostic procedures, while being cost-effective. In contrast, FPD Mobile C-arms feature flat-panel detector technology, providing superior image quality, lower radiation exposure, and enhanced versatility for complex procedures. Both types serve essential roles in various medical environments, such as hospitals and outpatient facilities, facilitating real-time imaging during surgeries.</p></p>
