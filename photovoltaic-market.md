<p><p>The photovoltaic market comprises three primary types: thin-film, monocrystalline (Mono Si), and polycrystalline (Multi Si) solar panels. Thin-film panels are lightweight and flexible, ideal for applications with space constraints, although they generally have lower efficiency. Monocrystalline panels boast the highest efficiency and lifespan, making them suitable for residential and commercial installations. In contrast, polycrystalline panels are more affordable and easier to produce, providing a good balance of cost and efficiency, making them popular for large-scale projects.</p></p>
