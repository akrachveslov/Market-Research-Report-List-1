<p><p>The Carrier Ethernet Equipment market encompasses three primary types: LAN (Local Area Network), MAN (Metropolitan Area Network), and WAN (Wide Area Network). LAN focuses on connecting devices within a limited area, such as an office or campus, offering high-speed connectivity. MAN extends this connectivity across a city or large campus, enabling efficient data transfer between multiple LANs. WAN covers broader geographical areas, linking networks across cities or countries, facilitating long-distance communication and robust internet access for enterprises and service providers.</p></p>
