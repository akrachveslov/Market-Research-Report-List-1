<p><p>The small rotary drilling rig market is categorized primarily into two types: crawler and rubber-tired rigs. Crawler rigs are equipped with tracks, providing superior stability and traction on uneven terrain, making them ideal for difficult ground conditions. In contrast, rubber-tired rigs use wheels for mobility, offering greater speed and flexibility on paved roads and smooth surfaces. These distinctions influence their applications in construction, mining, and environmental projects, catering to varying operational needs and site characteristics.</p></p>
