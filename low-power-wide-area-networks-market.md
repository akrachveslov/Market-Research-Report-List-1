<p><p>The Low-Power Wide Area Network (LPWAN) market is primarily categorized into three types: 3GPP, LoRa Alliance, and Others. The 3GPP segment focuses on standardized cellular technologies like NB-IoT and LTE-M, which provide reliable connectivity using existing mobile infrastructure. The LoRa Alliance promotes LoRaWAN, an open-source protocol designed for long-range, low-power communication, ideal for IoT applications. The "Others" market encompasses various proprietary and emerging technologies, catering to niche use cases and innovative solutions in the LPWAN ecosystem.</p></p>
