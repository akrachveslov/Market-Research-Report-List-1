<p><p>The blood pressure (BP) machines market is primarily divided into two types: wrist measurement and arm measurement devices. Wrist measurement devices are compact and convenient, allowing users to check their BP easily, though they can be less accurate if not positioned correctly. Arm measurement devices, often considered more reliable, use a cuff inflating around the upper arm to provide precise readings. Both types cater to varying user preferences, convenience needs, and accuracy levels, driving their distinct market segments.</p></p>
