<p><strong>The Galvanized Structure Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrical Galvanized Structure Steel</li><li>Hot-dip Galvanized Structure Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The galvanized structure steel market is primarily categorized into two types: electrical galvanized structure steel and hot-dip galvanized structure steel. Electrical galvanized steel involves a zinc coating applied through electrolysis, offering a thin layer of protection ideal for applications requiring precise finishes. In contrast, hot-dip galvanized structure steel is immersed in molten zinc, providing a robust and durable coating suitable for outdoor and aggressive environments. Both types enhance corrosion resistance, extending the lifespan of steel structures in various applications.</p></p>
