<p><p>The High Voltage Charge Pump market is characterized by two main types: Dual Supply/Output and Mono Supply. Dual Supply/Output charge pumps provide two voltage outputs, allowing versatility for applications requiring both positive and negative voltages, ideal for powering amplifiers or display drivers. In contrast, Mono Supply charge pumps deliver a single output voltage, which is suitable for simpler applications needing a consistent voltage boost. Both types enhance efficiency in power management across various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
