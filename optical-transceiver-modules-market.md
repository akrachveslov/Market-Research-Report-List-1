<p><p>The optical transceiver modules market encompasses various types including SFP, SFP+, QSFP/QSFP+, XFP, and CFP/CXP. SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) is widely used for data rates up to 1 Gbps, while SFP+ supports up to 10 Gbps. QSFP (Quad Small Form-factor Pluggable) and its enhanced version QSFP+ cater to higher data rates, typically 40 Gbps and above. XFP is designed for 10 Gbps applications, and CFP/CXP supports even higher capacities, making them essential for advanced networking and data center solutions.</p></p>
