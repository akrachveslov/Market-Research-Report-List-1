<p><ul><li>'Single Channel Pipettes'</li><li>'Multichannel Pipettes'</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic pipette systems market encompasses primarily two types: single-channel and multichannel pipettes. Single-channel pipettes are designed for precise and accurate dispensing of liquids in a one-at-a-time manner, ideal for applications requiring exact volumes and high precision. In contrast, multichannel pipettes feature multiple channels that allow simultaneous liquid handling, streamlining workflows for high-throughput tasks in laboratories, such as sample preparation and assays. Both types enhance efficiency and reproducibility in scientific research and clinical applications.</p></p>
