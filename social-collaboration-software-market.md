<p><p>Social collaboration software is categorized into three main types: on-premises, cloud-based, and web-based. On-premises social collaboration software is installed locally within an organization’s IT infrastructure, offering greater control and security but requiring significant maintenance. Cloud-based social collaboration software is hosted off-site and accessible via the internet, allowing for scalability and ease of updates. Web-based social collaboration software operates directly in a web browser, providing flexibility and ease of access across devices without the need for installations.</p></p>
