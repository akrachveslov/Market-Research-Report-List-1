<p><p>Emotion Artificial Intelligence (AI) is categorized into two primary market types: touch-based and touchless. Touch-based systems rely on physical interaction, utilizing devices like wearables or touchscreens to gauge user emotions through biometric feedback, such as heart rate or skin conductance. In contrast, touchless systems operate without physical contact, employing techniques like facial recognition, voice analysis, or gesture detection to assess emotional states. Both types aim to enhance user experience by understanding and responding to emotional cues effectively.</p></p>
