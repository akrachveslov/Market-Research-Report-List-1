<p><p>The DVI cable market is categorized into three main types: DVI-A, DVI-D, and DVI-I. DVI-A (Analog) transmits only analog signals and is compatible with VGA devices. DVI-D (Digital) carries purely digital signals, ensuring superior quality for digital displays. DVI-I (Integrated) supports both analog and digital signals, offering versatility in connecting various devices. Each type serves different needs in multimedia and display scenarios, influencing applications in consumer electronics, computing, and professional video setups.</p></p>
