<p><p>The salon hair dye market comprises three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dye lasts for a short duration, typically washing out after a few shampoos, making it ideal for quick changes. Semi-permanent dye offers more longevity, typically lasting several weeks, and gradually fades over time. Permanent hair dye provides long-lasting color, chemically altering the hair structure, ensuring the color remains until new hair growth occurs. Each type caters to different customer needs for color and maintenance.</p></p>
