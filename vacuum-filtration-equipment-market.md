<p><p>The vacuum filtration equipment market encompasses various types based on operational mechanisms. Mechanical vacuum filtration utilizes physical force, employing pumps to create a vacuum for solid-liquid separation, suitable for industrial applications. Electronic vacuum filtration integrates advanced automation and control features, enhancing precision and efficiency, often used in laboratories. Gas-phase vacuum filtration involves the removal of contaminants from gases, being essential in chemical processing and environmental applications, ensuring air quality and safety by filtering airborne particles and pollutants.</p></p>
