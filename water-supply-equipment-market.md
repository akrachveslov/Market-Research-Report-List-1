<p><ul><li>No Negative Pressure Frequency Conversion</li><li>Non-Tower Frequency Conversion</li><li>Dual Mode Variable Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water supply equipment market comprises various types, including No Negative Pressure Frequency Conversion, Non-Tower Frequency Conversion, and Dual Mode Variable Frequency systems. No Negative Pressure Frequency Conversion ensures consistent water supply without causing pressure drops. Non-Tower Frequency Conversion eliminates the need for traditional water towers by using pump systems to maintain pressure. Dual Mode Variable Frequency combines both constant and variable frequency operations to optimize efficiency based on demand, enhancing energy savings and overall system reliability in water supply management.</p></p>
