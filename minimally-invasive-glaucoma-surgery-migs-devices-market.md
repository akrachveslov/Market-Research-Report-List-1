<p><p>Minimally Invasive Glaucoma Surgery (MIGS) devices are innovative surgical instruments that facilitate the treatment of glaucoma with reduced trauma to the eye compared to traditional surgical methods. The growing prevalence of glaucoma, an age-related disease that poses significant health risks, is driving this market's expansion.</p><p>The MIGS devices market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.4% during the forecast period. Collaboration among manufacturers, healthcare providers, and research institutions is fostering innovation, leading to the introduction of new devices with improved efficacy and safety profiles.
