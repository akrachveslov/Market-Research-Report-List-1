<p><p>Gastric lavage, also known as stomach pumping, is a medical procedure used to remove the contents of the stomach, typically in cases of poisoning or overdose. This procedure is critical in emergency medical situations to minimize potential harm from ingested toxins.</p><p>The gastric lavage market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing incidences of poisoning and drug overdoses globally.
