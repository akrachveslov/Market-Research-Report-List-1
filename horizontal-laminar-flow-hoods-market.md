<p><p>Horizontal laminar flow hoods are essential in maintaining a sterile environment for laboratory and clinical applications. The market for these hoods is primarily segmented by filter type: HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) and ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters. HEPA filters capture particles as small as 0.3 micrometers, making them suitable for standard applications. In contrast, ULPA filters provide even higher filtration efficiency, filtering out particles down to 0.12 micrometers, ideal for more stringent cleanliness standards in specialized laboratories.</p></p>
