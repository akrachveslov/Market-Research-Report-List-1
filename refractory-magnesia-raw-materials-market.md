<p><p>The refractory magnesia raw materials market is primarily categorized into three types: Fused Magnesia, Dead Burned Magnesia, and Others. Fused Magnesia is produced by melting magnesite in an electric arc furnace, offering high purity and density, ideal for various refractories. Dead Burned Magnesia is obtained through the calcination of magnesite under high temperatures, providing excellent thermal stability for demanding applications. The "Others" category includes various specialty magnesia products used in niche applications, enhancing the overall versatility of the market.</p></p>
