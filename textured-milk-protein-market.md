<p><p>The textured milk protein market is divided into two main types: powder and liquid. Powdered textured milk proteins are processed to provide a dehydrated form, making them ideal for convenience and long shelf life, commonly used in protein supplements, baked goods, and snacks. Liquid textured milk proteins retain moisture and are often utilized in ready-to-drink beverages, dairy products, and culinary applications, offering a smooth texture and enhanced protein content. Both forms cater to diverse consumer preferences and applications in the food industry.</p></p>
