<p><p>The hydraulic rescue spreader market is primarily divided into single acting and double acting hydraulic rescue spreaders. Single acting spreaders use hydraulic pressure to open jaws, relying on a spring mechanism for closure, making them simpler and lighter. In contrast, double acting spreaders operate with hydraulic pressure for both opening and closing actions, providing more power and control during rescues. Both types are essential for emergency services, with specific applications based on the required strength and versatility.</p></p>
