<p><p>The 3D Time-of-Flight Sensor market comprises various types, primarily including Continuous Wave Measurement, Pulse Measurement, and Others. Continuous Wave Measurement relies on continuous light emission to gauge distance, providing accurate and smooth data for applications like gesture recognition. Pulse Measurement, on the other hand, emits short light pulses, measuring the time it takes for reflections to return, ideal for long-range scanning. The 'Others' category encompasses hybrid technologies and specialized sensors, catering to unique applications in industries like robotics, automotive, and gaming.</p></p>
