<p><p>The Tubing Pup Joint market is segmented into hot rolled and cold rolled types based on the manufacturing process. Hot rolled tubing involves heating metal above its recrystallization temperature, allowing for easier shaping and reducing internal stresses. This method often results in a rougher surface finish but is cost-effective for larger volumes. Conversely, cold rolled tubing is processed at room temperature, offering superior dimensional accuracy and a smoother finish, making it ideal for applications requiring higher precision and strength.</p></p>
