<p><ul><li>Internally Cooled Cryo Traps</li><li>Externally Cooled Cryo Traps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cryo Traps market is divided into two main types: internally cooled and externally cooled cryo traps. Internally cooled cryo traps utilize refrigeration techniques within the device to achieve low temperatures and trap volatile substances effectively. In contrast, externally cooled cryo traps rely on external cooling sources, such as liquid nitrogen or cryogenic fluids, to achieve the desired temperature. Both types serve vital roles in applications like gas analysis, vacuum systems, and freeze-drying, ensuring efficient collection of condensable vapors.</p></p>
