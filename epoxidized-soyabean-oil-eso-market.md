<p><p>Epoxidized Soyabean Oil (ESO) is a versatile bio-based chemical derived from soybean oil through an epoxidation process, introducing epoxide groups into the molecular structure. ESO is also increasingly recognized for its environmentally friendly attributes, making it a preferred choice in industries focusing on sustainability.</p><p>The ESO market is witnessing significant growth due to rising demand from industries such as automotive, construction, and packaging. Factors contributing to this growth include the increasing focus on non-toxic and biodegradable alternatives to conventional plasticizers and the expanding usage of ESO in coatings, adhesives, and sealants.
