<p><p>Gas scrubbers are essential devices for removing pollutants from industrial emissions. The wet type scrubber employs a liquid solution to absorb contaminants, effectively reducing harmful gases and particulate matter. This method is efficient for high levels of pollutants, but requires proper wastewater treatment. In contrast, the dry type scrubber utilizes solid materials, such as lime or activated carbon, to capture pollutants without producing wastewater. It is often more compact and easier to maintain, making it suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
